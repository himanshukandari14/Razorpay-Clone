    FAQs

    What is Razorpay Payment Link?
    What is Razorpay Payment Link?

    Razorpay Payment Links are an easy way to receive payments for goods and services. You enter in the name of the customer and the amount due and send the generated link to the person who needs to pay you.

    What can I do with a Razorpay Payment Link?
    What can I do with a Razorpay Payment Link?

    Razorpay Payment Links is a faster way to collect money into your bank account from your customers. On clicking the link, the customer can choose from the payment modes and complete payment accordingly. On payment completion, you will receive the amount in your bank account

    How can I send a Razorpay Payment Link?
    How can I send a Razorpay Payment Link?

    You can send a Razorpay Payment Link to your customer via email, SMS, Whatsapp and Facebook Messenger.

    How do I make a Razorpay Payment Link?
    How do I make a Razorpay Payment Link?

    You can create and send Razorpay Payment Links on the Razorpay Dashboard in 3 easy steps.

    1. Go to the Payment Links tab on your Razorpay Dashboard
    2. Click on 'Create Payment Link' button
    3. Fill customer information and amount requested
    4. Send it

    Can I send Payment Links in bulk?
    Can I send Payment Links in bulk?

    Yes, you can send Payment Links in bulk with the Batch Uploads feature. You can upload a CSV file containing the list of customers, amount requested and their contact details and send the requests in bulk.

    What are the advantages of a Payment Link
    What are the advantages of a Payment Link

    There are clear advantages to this method. You don’t need to rent the card processing machine and you don’t need to have a website. Customers don’t need to have a special account anywhere, they can just use their preferred payment option through the payment link

    4. Send it

    Can I send Payment Links in bulk?

    Yes, you can send Payment Links in bulk with the Batch Uploads feature. You can upload a CSV file containing the list of customers, amount requested and their contact details and send the requests in bulk.
